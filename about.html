About Us

About our Company

A solid partner for our customers and stakeholders.

Northwind suppliers and contractors limited is a %100 Zambian Owned Company incorporated in 2011. We are specialized in supply of various equipement and general contracting in the mining,construction energy and processing industries.
For over 15 years, we have built a strong reputation with our customers by consistently delivering high-quality products and exceptional services. At Northwind, we are committed to excellence in customer service and always prioritize our customers' needs.
Our extensive industry knowledge, strict adherence to industry standards, and expertise across various fields ensure that we provide only the best products and services.
Our mission is to contribute to the growth of the industries and communities we serve by delivering superior products, outstanding services, and creating employment opportunities for local talent.
